Progress

So in the last few weeks I have been writing what you could call my debut Novel, which if you didn't know was or is pretty much what I would consider my personal favourite or even my dreaded Magnum Opus. Writing is never easy but by now I have managed to at least write almost 110 page's which is a fair feat, regarding my current position and situation. If you have found this blog you have probably noted that I have already got a blog. Although with this being said I intend to use this blog as a method of easing some of the eventual memory that I will use up on the wordpress site. When you consider the free memory you get you have to compromise. So this is in theory my compromise.
